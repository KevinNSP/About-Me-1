Education

Carlo got his education from various institutions around the world. He started his academic career at Sullivan Pre School in Yokosuka Japan. From there he traveled many miles to California to attend RJ Neutra Elementary. He spend two years there before moving to Washington to attend three more elementary schools before attending Fairview Jr High and Olympic High School in Bremerton. Thinking that he needed to add more schools to his collection he decided to attend two comunity colleges and the University of Washington. Carlo must really love school because now he has decided to attend Code Fellows to continue his education.

Work Experience

Carlo has had a lot of work experience in many different fields. He has worked for retail companies like Factory 2-U, GAP, Abercrombie and Fitch and Game Crazy. He has also worked for marketing firms such as Worktank, and MRSC. For the past few years Carlo has been working in the restaurant industry for several Seattle restaurants such as Umi Sake House, Kushibar and I Love Sushi on Lake Union. Other jobs he's had include reporting for the UW Daily newspaper and parking attendant for Macy's.
